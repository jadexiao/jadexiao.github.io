I am a PhD candidate in Operations Research at the [H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering](https://www.isye.gatech.edu) at the Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by Professors [Turgay Ayer](https://sites.gatech.edu/turgay-ayer/) (Georgia Tech) and [Jagpreet Chhatwal](https://www.mgh-ita.org/profile/jag-chhatwal/) (MGH Institute for Technology Assessment).

I specialize in health economics and outcomes research (HEOR) with technical expertise in simulation modeling and cost-effectiveness analysis.

My doctoral thesis comprises three current and important pandemics: COVID-19, non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD), and opioid use disorder. See [`/publications`](/publications) for details!
