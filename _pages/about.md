I am a recent PhD graduate in Operations Research from Georgia Tech, where I was advised by Professors [Turgay Ayer](https://sites.gatech.edu/turgay-ayer/) and [Jagpreet Chhatwal](https://www.mgh-ita.org/profile/jag-chhatwal/).

I specialize in health economics and outcomes research (HEOR) with technical expertise in simulation modeling and cost-effectiveness analysis.

My doctoral thesis comprises three current and important pandemics: COVID-19, nonalcoholic fatty liver disease, and opioid addiction. See [`/publications`](/publications) for details!
